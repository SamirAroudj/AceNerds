# AceNerds

This is a collection of all the free AceNerds content units.
If you are interested in more context about the context units, have a look at our [knowledge library](https://www.acenerds.com/learning).

## Content Units

### Advanced Content Engines (ACEs)

1) If you want to know how to make best use of our [ACEs](https://www.acenerds.com/learning),
  first know what to expect and the [conventions the ACEs are based on](https://github.com/SamirAroudj/AceNerds/blob/main/ACEs/01Conventions/README.md).

### Software

1) Setup steps for
  [C++ code development under Windows](https://github.com/SamirAroudj/AceNerds/blob/main/Software/01VSCodeMSYS2ClangWindows/README.md)
  with VSCode, Clang & MSYS2.
2) Managing dependencies of C/C++ and CMake projects using
  ["automatic fetching"](https://github.com/SamirAroudj/AceNerds/blob/main/Software/02DependencyManagement/README.md).
3) If your 3D assets or transforms look off,
   have a look at why 3D assets and software tend to be plagued by
   [conventional chaos](https://github.com/SamirAroudj/AceNerds/tree/main/Software/03TheMessOf3DBases#blender-and-lbu).

### Business

1) Steps for [getting your startup going](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1OOGfT6M_Hd_OEqNcU7idiby1HaeO4v8fJ2XDrVAGhiQ/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.h1wam54cdpue).
2) Providing [value](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1rgr6jjKIMwsdodlT1X8-bdobnlrWN1X0mP9Dr9-Ed3o/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.z252zsuctylu) to customers.
